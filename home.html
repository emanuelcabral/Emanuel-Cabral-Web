About Me

Who I Am and What I Do

Full Stack Developer | Professor | AI Engineer

I am a web developer, AI Engineer, and technology instructor with over 10 years of experience in the digital field and more than 5 years training professionals. I currently teach at Coderhouse, where I teach Artificial Intelligence, Python, Web Development, WordPress, and No-Code.

I am a course creator in Frontend, Artificial Intelligence, and Python, and I specialize in educational content creation, including presentations, videos, PDFs, and hands-on challenges.

I work with technologies such as HTML, CSS, SASS, Bootstrap, JavaScript, Python, and SQL, as well as tools like Git and GitHub. I also have experience with databases (MySQL and PostgreSQL) and digital design, which allows me to approach projects in a comprehensive way.

I specialize in artificial intelligence, especially generative models, machine learning, and practical applications in different formats, developing solutions applied to real-world problems and supporting students in their preparation for the job market.
